About Coding For Kids

Coding for kids is an afterschool class for grades 4-5. Our unit covers the very basics of web-design. Students will learn the basics of HTML5, CSS3, Animate.css, Bootstrap, and touch upon fundamental jQuery concepts. The majority of the unit focuses on markup languages, but students that learn to understand concepts and topics quickly can move onto supervised Javascript projects.

This website serves the purpose of providing useful information to students, so they can work at home, and it gives parents the oppertunity to see how their children are doing by checking their project page.

What makes Coding For Kids different?

When I first taught this program last year, we used the simple click and drag programs that are ubiquitous on the internet. I tried them myself, and after completing their entire program, I thought to myself, "Wait a minute, I spent two weeks clicking and dragging things, and I still have no idea how to code."

After realizing that I didn't develop any skills doing that, it became rather obvious that the students didn't either, even though this was the system that was being adopted by public schools. So I decided to make my own curriculum, and at the next class, I taught students how to write their very own code, using the same software that actual web-developers use. Since then, I've learned more about coding everyday, and I wake up excited to share this knowledge with my students.

What makes this class different is that kids walk away from the unit, with a new skill, and know where to go to get better, and the best part is, every students website is completely unique! The students tell me what they want to their website to look like or do, and we work together on making that happen! It's the perfect creative outlet for young students!

How should I use this site?

Parents, you can use this site to check out your childs progress! All student websites will be hosted right here! To find your child's website, select Projects on the top menu or click here!

Students, I will host anything you create here for as long as you'd like! If you create something five days or even five years from now and would like to host it online, feel free to contact me and I will upload your code to this website.
